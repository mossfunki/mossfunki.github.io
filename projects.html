Austin Multifamily Investment Scorer

A GIS-driven scoring model that ranks Austin census tracts by multifamily investment potential.

Project overview

This project builds a quantitative investment score for every census tract in Austin, TX. The score blends demographics, rental demand, and proximity to downtown to highlight high- potential locations for multifamily development.

What the model uses

  • Median income and rent levels from Census / ACS data
  • Renter share of households as a proxy for demand
  • Distance to downtown Austin and major job centers
  • Normalised composite score on a 0–100 scale

Tools & stack

  • Python, Pandas, GeoPandas, NumPy
  • Folium for interactive web mapping
  • Matplotlib for distribution and correlation plots
  • Public census & demographic datasets
