Commercial Real Estate Investment Scorer

Project Overview

A GIS-based tool that identifies high-potential multifamily investment opportunities in Austin, TX using economic and demographic data analysis.

Key Insights

  • Identified 15 high-potential census tracts with investment scores > 80
  • Strong correlation between income levels and investment potential
  • Location within 5 miles of downtown showed 40% higher average scores
