Austin Investment Scores

Visualization and presentation of neighborhood-level investment scores derived from the composite analysis.

Purpose

These visuals were designed to communicate scoring outcomes clearly to non-technical stakeholders, supporting prioritization and discussion rather than prescriptive ranking.

Design Choices

  • Clear ordering with limited color palette
  • Emphasis on relative differences, not absolute values
  • Legibility at presentation and print scales
Visualization tools
Python · Matplotlib
Adobe Illustrator (final polish)
