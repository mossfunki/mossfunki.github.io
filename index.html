Transit-Oriented Development & Affordability Model

Regression model quantifying the price premium of proximity to transit across 531k King County parcels. Spatial joins link parcel records to GTFS stop distances, walk-score buffers, and zoning overlays. OLS with spatial lag controls for neighborhood autocorrelation. Identifies where affordability gaps are widest relative to transit access — actionable for planners and housing policy.
