Austin Investment Opportunity

A single applied GIS project that develops a neighborhood-level investment score for Austin and delivers results through an interactive web map supported by analytical figures.

Role: Data & GIS analysis · Tools: ArcGIS Pro, Python, Web Mapping

Context & Method

This project was built to explore spatial patterns in investment potential across Austin neighborhoods. Multiple demographic, accessibility, and built-environment indicators were standardized and combined into a composite score designed for exploratory analysis and prioritization.

  • Compiled indicators from census, land use, and accessibility data
  • Standardized variables and constructed a composite score
  • Tested sensitivity across alternative weighting schemes
  • Delivered results through an interactive web map

Outcome & Use

Together, the interactive map and supporting analysis revealed clear spatial clustering of high investment potential near transit corridors and mixed-use areas. The scoring approach remained stable across reasonable weighting choices, supporting its use as an exploratory and prioritization tool rather than a definitive ranking.
