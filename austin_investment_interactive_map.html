Austin Investment Opportunity Map

An applied GIS project that combines spatial indicators into a neighborhood-level investment score, presented through an interactive map and supporting visuals.

Overview

This project was developed to help identify neighborhoods in Austin with strong investment potential while balancing accessibility, land use, and socioeconomic context. The goal was transparency, interpretability, and spatial clarity.

Methodology

  • Compiled demographic, land-use, and accessibility indicators
  • Standardized variables and constructed a composite score
  • Validated results through sensitivity analysis
  • Delivered results via interactive web mapping
Tools & stack
GIS · Spatial Analysis · Python
ArcGIS Pro · Web Mapping
Census & open spatial data

Outcome & Use

The project revealed clear spatial clustering of high investment potential near transit corridors and mixed-use areas. Score rankings remained stable across reasonable weighting variations, supporting use as an exploratory and prioritization tool rather than a prescriptive ranking.
