Austin Investment Opportunity Map

An applied GIS case study combining spatial indicators into a neighborhood-level investment score, delivered through an interactive web map plus supporting visuals.

Problem

Decision-makers needed a transparent, spatially explicit way to identify neighborhoods with high investment potential while balancing socioeconomic, infrastructural, and accessibility indicators.

Approach

  • Compiled demographic, land-use, and accessibility indicators
  • Standardized variables and constructed a composite score
  • Mapped results using an interactive web interface
Tools & stack
ArcGIS Pro · Python (pandas, geopandas)
Web mapping (Leaflet / Mapbox)
Census & open spatial data

Outcome

The map revealed clear spatial clusters of high investment potential near transit corridors and mixed-use areas. Sensitivity testing showed score stability across reasonable weighting schemes, supporting its use for exploratory analysis and prioritization.
