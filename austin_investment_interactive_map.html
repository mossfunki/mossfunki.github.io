Austin Investment Opportunity Map

An applied GIS project that produces a neighborhood-level investment score and an interactive web map for exploration and stakeholder briefings. Below the map you'll find the two supporting figures used in analysis and presentation.

Role: Data & GIS lead · Tools: ArcGIS Pro · Python · Leaflet/Mapbox · QGIS

Overview & Method

The project compiles demographic, built-environment, and accessibility indicators, standardizes them, and combines them into a composite investment score at the neighborhood scale. Results are presented via an interactive map plus two concise figures used in briefings.

  • Data: ACS, OpenStreetMap, local parcel & land-use data
  • Processing: Python (pandas, geopandas), spatial joins, zonal statistics
  • Mapping: lightweight web map with responsive popups and decile filters
Deliverables
Interactive map, score summary figure, analysis figure, reproducible scripts

Outcome & Use

The map and supporting figures were used for stakeholder briefings and exploratory prioritization. Top deciles align with transit corridors and mixed-use nodes; the scoring approach performed robustly under alternative weighting schemes.
